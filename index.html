This script creates a folder named 'GmailAttachments' in your GDrive and stores all the files in it.


It runs periodically in the background and keeps updating your GDrive with the new attachments that you have received.

How would you like the folder structure under GmailAttachments to look like?

Arrange by content type. (eg. One folder for pdfs, one for images, one for audio etc.)
Arrange by month.
Arrange by year. (All attachments received in a month will be put in a folder by that month and year name).

For first sync, how many years of Gmail attachments you want in your Drive?

Past 1 year
Past 2 years
Past 4 years
Past 8 year
All mails. (Goes only upto past 10 years).
